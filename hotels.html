Unwind in a spacious guest room and enjoy breathtaking views of the lake or the surrounding mountainous desert. Refresh in the marble bathroom, watch movies on the 42-inch HDTV and check emails with complimentary WiFi. Upgrade to a suite for an expansive lounge."
